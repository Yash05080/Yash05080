## About

Final year Information Technology student at **IIIT Una**, currently interning as an SDE at **Amazon**. I build production-grade mobile apps and distributed backend systems — primarily with Flutter and Go.

I shipped **[Hangukverse](https://play.google.com/store/apps)** — a full-stack Korean entertainment platform — independently from zero to launch on both the Play Store and App Store for a client.
